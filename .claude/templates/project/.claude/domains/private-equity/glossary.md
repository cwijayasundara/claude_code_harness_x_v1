# Private-equity glossary

Populate and approve terms with domain owners. Keep this file client-specific and free of confidential deal data.

| Term | Meaning | Related terms |
|---|---|---|
| Fund | Investment vehicle governed by defined terms. | Investor, Investment, NAV |
| Commitment | Amount an investor agrees to contribute. | Capital call, unfunded commitment |
| Capital call | Request for committed capital subject to valid lifecycle and amount rules. | Commitment, distribution |
| Distribution | Payment from a fund to an investor. | Investment, waterfall |
| NAV | Net asset value at a stated valuation point. | Valuation, fund |
